About Me

I graduated from Oberlin College in 2024 with a double major in Creative Writing and Cinema Studies and a minor in Music, Phi Beta Kappa. I was honored with the Stuart Friebert and Diane Vreuls Prize for excellence in creative writing, and my prose-poem published in The Fabulist received a Pushcart nomination. After graduating, I worked as an admissions counselor at Oberlin for a year. Now I work as an assistant to the college’s multifaith chaplain, especially focusing on facilitating dialogue across social differences.


This website serves as a comprehensive portfolio of my writing across medium and genre. If you have any questions or are looking for any other samples, please reach out to me at scolby@oberlin.edu.
